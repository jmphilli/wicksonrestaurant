Wickson is a neighborhood, wood-fired restaurant open for dinner and weekend lunches within Anderson Valley’s The Madrones.





We work directly with farmers of Mendocino and Sonoma Counties, fishers and foragers of the Northern California coast, and brewers and vintners of Anderson Valley to create a seasonal, plant-forward menu.
